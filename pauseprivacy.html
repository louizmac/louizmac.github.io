The "pause" app (referred to as "the App") is built as a minimalist utility timer. This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the App.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The App is designed to run entirely locally on your device. It does not require a user account, and it does not transmit any information over the internet.

2. Local Data Storage

Any configuration or data you interact with remains strictly on your device:

  • Timer Settings: Your preferred timer durations (hours, minutes, seconds) and display formats are saved locally on your device using Apple's @AppStorage system.
  • Live Activities: The dynamic timer updates shown on your Lock Screen or Dynamic Island are processed entirely by iOS locally.

3. Device Permissions

To provide its core functionality, the App may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: Used solely to send you a local alert when your timer has finished. This notification is triggered directly by your device's operating system, and no data is sent to an external server.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools (such as Firebase or Google Analytics), or advertising networks.
